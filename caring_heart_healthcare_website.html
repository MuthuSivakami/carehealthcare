What We Offer

Our Care Services

From home nursing to alternative medicine, we offer a wide range of compassionate healthcare services tailored to your needs.

🏠

Home Nursing Care

Professional nurses visit your home to provide post-operative care, wound dressing, medication management, and daily health monitoring.

🌿

Alternative Medicine

Holistic healing treatments including naturopathy, acupressure, and herbal therapy to complement modern medical care.

👴

Elder Care Services

Compassionate support for senior citizens with daily activities, personal hygiene, nutrition monitoring, and companionship.

💉

Injection & IV Therapy

Safe and sterile administration of injections, intravenous fluids, and other prescribed medical procedures at home.

🫀

Vital Monitoring

Regular monitoring of blood pressure, blood sugar, oxygen saturation, and other critical health parameters.

🧘

Rehabilitation Care

Physiotherapy assistance and recovery support for stroke, fractures, and post-surgical rehabilitation programs.
